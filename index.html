How to play

  1. Set a bet from your balance, then guess HI or LO (50/50).
  2. Correct → your fighter hits and removes one of their yellow boxes; your multiplier climbs (about +15% per win — e.g. 1.15×, 1.32×, 1.52×…).
  3. Cash out anytime the multiplier is above 1x to bank bet × multiplier into your balance.
  4. Wrong → you lose the bet and one of your life boxes; multiplier resets to 1x.
  5. Empty their boxes → round clear. Your streak / climb stays in play — hit Keep fighting to refill life boxes and continue (or cash out).
  6. Empty yours → you lose the fight.
  7. Fairness: outcomes use a committed server-seed hash + your client seed + nonce (copy them below).
